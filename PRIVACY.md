# ZenTab Privacy Policy

**Last Updated**: February 8, 2026
**Effective Date**: February 8, 2026

## Overview

ZenTab ("we", "our", or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our Chrome extension.

## Data Collection

### What We Collect

ZenTab collects and stores the following data **locally on your device only**:

1. **Tab Management Data**
   - URLs of suspended tabs
   - Tab titles and favicons
   - Suspension timestamps
   - Whitelist domains

2. **User Preferences**
   - Auto-suspend timeout settings
   - Whitelist configurations
   - UI preferences (theme, language)

3. **Usage Statistics**
   - Number of tabs suspended
   - Estimated memory saved
   - Extension usage patterns

### What We DO NOT Collect

- ❌ Personal identification information
- ❌ Browsing history beyond active tabs
- ❌ Form data or passwords
- ❌ Credit card information
- ❌ Location data
- ❌ Analytics or tracking data

## Data Storage

All data is stored **locally** using Chrome's `chrome.storage.local` API. We do **NOT**:

- Upload data to external servers
- Share data with third parties
- Use analytics services (Google Analytics, etc.)
- Track user behavior

## Data Usage

Your data is used exclusively for:

1. Managing tab suspension/restoration
2. Applying your custom settings
3. Displaying memory savings statistics

## Third-Party Services

ZenTab does **NOT** integrate with any third-party services, APIs, or analytics platforms.

## Data Security

- All data remains on your local device
- No network requests are made (except loading tab content)
- No cookies or tracking mechanisms

## User Rights

You have full control over your data:

- **Access**: View all stored data via extension settings
- **Delete**: Clear all data by uninstalling the extension
- **Export**: Manually export whitelist/settings (future feature)

## Children's Privacy

ZenTab does not knowingly collect data from users under 13 years of age.
